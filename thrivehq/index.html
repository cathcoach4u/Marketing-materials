Where members go from here

ThriveHQ is not a course. It is a community with a direction. Most members move through three recognisable stages.

Stage 1

Scattered and Stuck

Life feels overwhelming and reactive. Hard to start, hard to finish, hard to know where to begin.

Stage 2

Naming What's Real

You start to notice patterns. You build self-awareness. The shame starts to loosen.

Stage 3

Settling Into Yourself

You feel more grounded and less self-critical. You have scaffolding that actually holds.

Most members arrive at Stage 1 or 2. The community meets you wherever you are.

Why I built ThriveHQ

I am not observing ADHD from a distance. I live it. I know the exhaustion of feeling constantly behind, the shame of another abandoned system, and the loneliness of thinking you are the only one who cannot seem to get it together.

For years, I tried to force my brain into systems designed for neurotypical people. I kept failing and blaming myself. Then I discovered strengths-based coaching and everything shifted. I stopped trying to fix what was "wrong" and started building scaffolding that worked with my brain, not against it.

Before moving into coaching and counselling, I spent years in financial planning and general insurance. I have always been drawn to the deeper questions — how people want to live, what gets in the way, and what really matters. That path led me to retrain in counselling and build a strengths-based approach that supports real, lasting change. My work is grounded in practical experience and a deep understanding of neurodiversity — through my own lived experience, and through walking alongside family members on their journey too.

ThriveHQ is the space I wish I had. Not a course. Not another productivity hack. A real community where you show up as you are — and that is enough.

Cath

A few honest questions

I always start things and stop. How is this different?

ThriveHQ is specifically designed for that pattern. Missing a session is expected — that is why there is no homework, no catch-up pressure, and no guilt. You just come back. The community will still be there.

My ADHD is not that severe. Is this still right for me?

If things still feel scattered and you know there has to be an easier way, you belong here. ThriveHQ is for anyone navigating ADHD — wherever they are on that journey.

What if I cannot make the session times?

Join when you can. Sessions are recorded where possible, and the WhatsApp group keeps you connected in between. Consistent participation matters more than perfect attendance.

Why a 6-month commitment?

Real change takes time. A 6-month membership gives you enough space to actually move through the stages — not just try something for a few weeks and call it a failure. It is also renewable.

Can I try FocusHQ first and move to ThriveHQ later?

Yes. FocusHQ is the body doubling tier — same 6-month commitment, lower investment. Upgrade at any time by reaching out to Cath directly.
