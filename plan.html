Brand Voice Rules

Do

  • Warm, professional, clear
  • Strengths-based, not clinical
  • Australian English
  • Short paragraphs, easy to scan
  • Practical over theoretical
  • Close emails with "Thanks"
  • One improvement at a time
  • Name the problem in plain language

Don't

  • No exclamation marks
  • No em dashes in prose
  • No clinical or therapy jargon
  • Never call Coach4U a psychology practice
  • No "Best regards" or "Cheers" sign-offs
  • No generic productivity advice
  • No American English
  • No purple gradients or emoji-heavy design
