# ADHD Materials — Sales Plan
**ThriveHQ / Coach4U | Cath Baker | May 2026**

---

## What to sell

Stick to products tied to your proprietary frameworks. Generic workbooks have been commoditised by AI — buyers know they can generate those themselves.

| Product | Format | Price (AUD) |
|---|---|---|
| ThriveHQ Brain Pulse self-assessment + band guide | PDF | $25 |
| SAFEty Pulse self-reflection worksheet | PDF | $18 |
| Strengths for ADHD workbook (Name-Claim-Aim applied to ADHD) | PDF | $35 |
| Weekly Reset planner (four ThriveHQ rooms as structure) | PDF | $15 |
| ThriveHQ Starter Kit (Brain Pulse + Weekly Reset + Purpose doc) | Bundle PDF | $49 |

ThriveHQ members: include all materials in membership at no extra cost. This adds perceived membership value and removes a competing purchase decision.

---

## Where to sell

**Recommended: Gumroad, embedded on coach4u.com.au**

Gumroad is purpose-built for solo practitioners selling digital downloads. It handles payment processing, delivery, and receipts automatically. You embed a button or product widget on your WordPress site — no plugin conflicts, no WooCommerce complexity.

Alternative: Payhip (similar feature set, slightly lower fees on higher volume).

Do not use Etsy — your products would compete with generic ADHD stationery and your brand positioning would be diluted.

---

## Pricing rationale

- $15-$35 for single items sits at an impulse-buy threshold for ADHD buyers (low friction to decide)
- $49 bundle creates a perceived discount and increases average order value
- These price points also position the materials as accessible, not premium — the premium offer is coaching itself

Do not undercut further. Below $15 signals low value and attracts buyers who are not your client profile.

---

## How to promote

**Phase 1 — Warm audience first (Month 1)**
- Offer to existing ThriveHQ members as complimentary resources (builds loyalty, tests the materials)
- Email your client list announcing availability
- Add a "Resources" page to coach4u.com.au with product listings

**Phase 2 — Referral and content (Month 2-3)**
- Write one short LinkedIn post per product — focus on the problem it solves, not the product itself
- Mention in MHPN neurodiversity facilitation contexts where appropriate
- Add a product mention to your email signature or consultation follow-up emails

**Phase 3 — Passive discovery (ongoing)**
- SEO-optimised product pages on your site
- Consider a free lead magnet (one short worksheet, free download) to grow your email list

## Realistic revenue model

| Scenario | Monthly sales | Revenue |

| Conservative | 8 items/month avg $25 | ~$200/month |
| Moderate | 20 items/month avg $28 | ~$560/month |

Passive income from downloads will always be secondary to coaching and ThriveHQ membership revenue. The stronger commercial case is using materials to convert browsers into clients and to increase ThriveHQ retention.
